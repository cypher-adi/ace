Who Are We Guys

We at ACE create a framework which helps propagate enthusiasm and knowledge in the field of civil engineering and to provide the launching platform to budding civil engineers. An opportunity for young guns enveloping the wide diversities of civil engineering - a pedestal to present ideas, a stage to showoff designs, a playpen to re-build the blocks

Also, through associating and helping execute the activities involved in this framework called the ACE, students get an opportunity to work in a professional environment and imbibe the core values that a leader possesses, namely, teamwork, camaderie, sincereity and puctuality, therefore strive to achieve the goals of ACE.
